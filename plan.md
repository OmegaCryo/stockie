# Project Overview

**Name:** Stockie

**Puprose:** Create a caculator base on the stock strategy "Buy In Value Zone"

**Problem:** There are many stragtegies when it comes to buying stock, but not a lot are beginner friendly. The "Buy In Value Zone" stragtegy can a simple way to buy stock base off zones on a particular. With the stock caculator you look up current stock you own and the caculator will take lowest stock price from the past six months and current high and create a range base the best price to buy stock.

**Goal:** A simple stock caculator that caculates the best buy zones for a user stock. It will caculate two zones Buy Zone (upper half) and Buy Zone (lower half).

**OutScope:** The caculator will only caculate base on last six months.Twelve months maybe in future updates.

**Target Users:** Beginner stock traders who don't have stock trader stragery yet.
