# Formula

## Data Definitions

- Ticker = User input (e.g.APPL)
- Current High (CH) = Today's high price
- Six month low (L) = Lowest daily low in the past 6 months
- Range (R) = CH - L

### Buy Zone Caculations

Slight Pull back

`Buy Zone 1 = CH(R x 0.382)`

Big Pull back

`Buy Zone 2 = CH(R x 0.618)`
